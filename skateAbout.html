about us

The idea for this project stemmed from my old skate boarding days. My friends and I would spend so much time looking for new spots to hit that some of them got bored and eventually gave up the hobby. To help keep this scene alive, I am creating a spot map and, hopefully, a community. One where everyone can come together for the same thing, to have fun.

- J.D.
